Search Tips

Scope

Choose which of the Library's sub-collections you would like to search.

Scope Includes
Articles, Books, and more Everything provided by all UC libraries plus others
UCSB Library Catalog Resources at UCSB, excluding articles
UCSB Course Reserves Resources reserved for UCSB courses
UCSB Special Research Collections Materials available by request for use in the reading room during posted hours

Operators

Enhance your search queries by combining the following operators with your terms:

Operator Description Example
AND Looks for records with fields that match terms on both sides of operator "author AND title", "title AND series"
OR Matches for one term or another author OR author
(parentheses) Establishes order of operations. The default is set from left to right. (author AND title) OR series
NOT Excludes results that match against a term author AND title NOT review

Filters

Use filters to narrow your list of results and to speed-up finding specific materials.
