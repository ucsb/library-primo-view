Welcome to the New UCSB Library Search

UCSB Library Search is a tool you can use to find books available in the UCSB Library, as well as other types of materials including:

  • Scholarly and Peer-Reviewed Articles
  • Journals
  • Dissertations
  • Media (Music, Films, Video)
  • Government Documents
  • Newspaper Articles
  • Reviews
  • Course Reserves

Using this single search box, discover a variety of sources with a click of a button! Then filter your results according to your research needs.
